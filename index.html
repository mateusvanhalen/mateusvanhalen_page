"Utripn"

Front End Capstone

with react.js

Utripn is an abbreviated journal app built in react.js that is friendly for users of ALL ages to share, recommend, and recap their trips of all shapes and sizes. Utripn solves the problem for bulky wordy journals and provides a quick and fun reference. Some add on features like picture uploading and a youtube reference video give some aesthetics and add more sensory reminders for the user. Link coming soon!
